Team Vision

The Foodie project team comprises six enthusiastic last-year college students who share a passion for building a web application that simplifies the process of purchasing food ticket online. Led by Arnab Dutta, the team members include Sourav Mukherjee, Soumyajit Mukherjee, Sahil Poddar, Subham Santra, and Subhra Mondal. While they may be relatively new to the industry and lack prior experience, they have a strong foundation in several essential programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and DBMS. The team is eager to leverage their knowledge and apply their skills to create something innovative that can make an impact in the food industry.
